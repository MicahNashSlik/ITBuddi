How We Transform Businesses

Real-world examples of our packages in action.

Micro Business

The Local Plumber

The Challenge: Relied 100% on word-of-mouth. Missed calls (and jobs) while on-site, and had zero online presence to capture new leads.

The ITbuddi Solution: Deployed our Kickstart Package. We built a professional 3-page website with a clear "Request a Quote" form and set up a Google Business Profile, optimized for local search.

The Transformation: Now appears in "plumber near me" searches. The website form captures leads 24/7, and they saw a 40% increase in qualified leads within 3 months.

Small Business

The Local Butcher Shop

The Challenge: Wanted to sell speciality meat packs online, but manual order-taking via WhatsApp was chaotic and led to errors.

The ITbuddi Solution: Implemented the Starter Package. We set up a Shopify store with a local payment gateway and added a basic AI chatbot to the site to answer FAQs like "Opening hours?".

The Transformation: Online orders are now streamlined, reducing admin errors. The chatbot handles 60% of routine queries, freeing up staff and expanding their customer base beyond their suburb.

Medium Business

The Security Services Co.

The Challenge: A growing sales team had inconsistent lead follow-up. The quoting process was slow, and they had no way to track which marketing efforts were working.

The ITbuddi Solution: Upgraded to the Growth Package. We set up a CRM to track all leads, automated email follow-ups, and an advanced AI chatbot that qualifies new leads by asking for service type and property size.

The Transformation: Lead response time was cut from 24 hours to 5 minutes. The sales team has a clear pipeline, and management can see exactly which marketing channels drive conversions via their custom dashboard.
